SUBJECT: Change Order for Modification in Elevator Interiors and Additional Features


1. BACKGROUND:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This Change Order is issued to incorporate certain modifications and upgrades in the elevator interiors and features for the Green Valley Residential Complex project, over and above the original scope defined in Purchase Order PO2026001.


2. DETAILS OF CHANGE:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

A. INTERIOR UPGRADE:
   - Change from hairline finish stainless steel panels to a combination of:
       • Mirror finish stainless steel for rear panel
       • Decorative laminate panels on side walls
   - Additional LED ceiling design with indirect lighting
   - Handrails upgraded to designer stainless steel profile

B. DISPLAY & CONTROL UPGRADE:
   - Upgraded full-color TFT display inside car
   - Touch-type car operating panel (COP)
   - Call registration with anti-nuisance feature

C. ADDITIONAL FEATURES:
   - Integration with building access control system (provision only)
   - Remote monitoring interface provision at security room

D. QUANTITY:
   - The above changes apply to all 8 elevators supplied under PO2026001.
