KEY DISCUSSION POINTS:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. PRICE NEGOTIATION:
   - Initial quoted basic amount (8 elevators): Rs. 1,24,00,000
   - Client requested a reduction of at least 5%
   - Vendor offered a 3% discount on basic value

   Outcome:
   - Agreed basic value after 3% discount:
     Rs. 1,24,00,000 – 3% = Rs. 1,20,28,000
   - GST @ 18%: Rs. 21,65,040
   - Revised Total (Incl. GST): Rs. 1,41,93,040

2. DELIVERY SCHEDULE:
   - Original offer: 10–12 weeks from PO
   - Client requested: 8–10 weeks due to project timeline
   - Vendor confirmed:
       • 10 weeks committed delivery for all 8 units
       • Priority dispatch for Tower A and B elevators

   Outcome:
   - Delivery milestone agreed at 10 weeks from PO date.

3. PAYMENT TERMS:
   - Original:
       • 20% advance
       • 60% on material delivery
       • 15% after installation and testing
       • 5% after final commissioning

   - Client requested lower advance and higher linkage to performance.

   Outcome (Revised Payment Terms):
       • 10% advance against PO
       • 60% on delivery at site
       • 20% after successful installation and testing
       • 10% after final commissioning and handover

4. WARRANTY & AMC:
   - Warranty offered: 24 months
   - Client requested: 24 months + discounted AMC

   Outcome:
       • Warranty: 24 months from handing over (unchanged)
       • Discounted comprehensive AMC:
         – 1st and 2nd year after warranty @ 3.5% of basic cost per year
         – Thereafter @ 4% of basic cost per year

5. TECHNICAL CLARIFICATIONS:
   - Compliance with Indian Lift Act and IS codes confirmed by vendor
   - All elevators to be supplied with ARD, overload sensors, and fire recall
   - Final shop drawings to be submitted within 2 weeks of PO

   - Payment Terms:                        10% / 60% / 20% / 10%
