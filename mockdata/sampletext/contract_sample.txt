ARTICLE 1: SCOPE OF WORK

1.1 The Contractor shall execute and complete the following works:
    a) Foundation and basement construction for 4 towers
    b) Structural work up to terrace level (G+15 floors)
    c) Brick masonry and plastering work
    d) Internal and external finishing work
    e) Plumbing and sanitary installations
    f) Electrical work including DB boards and wiring
    g) Fire fighting systems installation
    h) Lift installation (2 lifts per tower)

1.2 All work shall be executed as per approved drawings and specifications


ARTICLE 2: CONTRACT PRICE AND PAYMENT TERMS

2.1 Total Contract Value: INR 8,50,00,000

2.2 Payment Schedule:
    - Mobilization Advance: 10% (INR 85,00,000) against Bank Guarantee
    - On completion of Foundation: 25% (INR 2,12,50,000)
    - On completion of up to 7th floor: 25% (INR 2,12,50,000)  
    - On completion of up to 15th floor: 20% (INR 1,70,00,000)
    - On completion of finishing work: 15% (INR 1,27,50,000)
    - Final payment after defect liability: 5% (INR 42,50,000)

2.3 Retention: 10% shall be deducted from each running bill
     - 50% released on Completion Certificate
     - 50% released after 12 months defect liability period

2.4 Payment Timeline: All payments within 30 days of invoice submission

2.5 GST: Applicable GST (18%) shall be paid extra over contract value


ARTICLE 3: TIME FOR COMPLETION

3.1 Contract Duration: 18 months from commencement date

3.2 Milestone Schedule:
    - Foundation completion: Month 4
    - Structural work up to 7th floor: Month 10
    - Structural work completion: Month 14  
    - Finishing work completion: Month 17
    - Final handover: Month 18

3.3 Time is the essence of this contract


ARTICLE 4: LIQUIDATED DAMAGES

4.1 In case of delay in completion beyond stipulated time:

    Liquidated Damages shall be levied at 0.5% of contract value per week
    of delay, subject to maximum of 10% of total contract value.

4.2 Delays due to the following shall be excluded:
    - Force Majeure events
    - Employer's changes to scope  
    - Late supply of Employer's materials
    - Site access delays caused by Employer

4.3 LD calculation shall be on per week basis (7 days = 1 week)


ARTICLE 5: PERFORMANCE SECURITY

5.1 The Contractor shall submit Performance Security of 10% of Contract Value
    (INR 85,00,000) in the form of Bank Guarantee within 15 days of LOA

5.2 Performance Security shall be valid until 90 days after defect liability period

5.3 Performance Security may be encashed in case of:
    - Contractor's failure to commence work
    - Abandonment of work
    - Breach of contract terms
    - Non-rectification of defects


ARTICLE 6: INSURANCE

6.1 The Contractor shall obtain and maintain:
    a) Contractor's All Risk Insurance covering full contract value
    b) Third Party Liability Insurance - minimum INR 10 Crores
    c) Workmen's Compensation Insurance as per statutory requirements

6.2 Employer shall be named as co-insured on all policies

6.3 All insurance policies to be submitted within 7 days of contract signing


ARTICLE 7: QUALITY REQUIREMENTS  

7.1 All materials shall conform to IS specifications

7.2 All work shall be subject to quality inspection by Employer's consultant

7.3 Any defective work shall be rectified at Contractor's cost within 7 days

7.4 Penalty for quality non-compliance:
    - Minor defect: INR 50,000 per instance
    - Major defect: INR 2,00,000 per instance  
    - Critical safety violation: INR 5,00,000 per instance


ARTICLE 8: WARRANTY

8.1 Defect Liability Period: 12 months from Completion Certificate

8.2 Structural warranty: 5 years from final completion

8.3 MEP equipment warranty: As per manufacturer's warranty (min 1 year)


ARTICLE 9: TERMINATION

9.1 Employer may terminate this contract if Contractor:
    - Delays work by more than 60 consecutive days
    - Becomes bankrupt or insolvent  
    - Abandons the work
    - Commits serious breach of contract terms

9.2 Notice period: 14 days to remedy default, failing which immediate termination

9.3 On termination, Performance Security shall be forfeited


ARTICLE 10: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

10.1 Any disputes shall first be referred to Project Manager for resolution

10.2 If not resolved within 30 days, disputes shall be referred to Arbitration

10.3 Arbitration shall be as per Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996

10.4 Seat of Arbitration: Gurugram, Haryana


ARTICLE 11: FORCE MAJEURE

11.1 Force Majeure events include: War, riots, earthquake, floods, epidemics,
      government orders, strikes (not related to Contractor's labor)

11.2 Party affected shall notify other party within 7 days

11.3 Time extension shall be granted for Force Majeure delays

11.4 If Force Majeure continues beyond 180 days, either party may terminate
